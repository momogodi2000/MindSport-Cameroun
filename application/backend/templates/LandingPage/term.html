Privacy Policy

At MindSport Cameroon, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mental health platform for combat athletes.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, date of birth, and other contact details when you register.
  • Health Information: Mental health assessments, session notes (only with your consent), and wellness tracking data.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with our platform, including IP addresses, device information, and browsing patterns.
  • Payment Information: When you purchase services, we collect necessary billing details (processed securely by our payment partners).

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain our mental health services
  • Connect you with appropriate mental health professionals
  • Personalize your experience and improve our platform
  • Process transactions and send service-related communications
  • Comply with legal obligations and protect against fraudulent activity

3. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • All health data is encrypted both in transit and at rest
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability testing
  • Strict access controls to ensure only authorized personnel can view sensitive data
  • Compliance with Cameroonian data protection regulations

4. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access, update, or delete your personal information
  • Request a copy of your health records
  • Withdraw consent for data processing (where applicable)
  • Lodge a complaint with regulatory authorities

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes through our platform or via email.

6. Contact Us

For questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact our Data Protection Officer at privacy@mindsport.cm.

Terms of Service

By accessing or using the MindSport Cameroon platform, you agree to be bound by these Terms of Service. Please read them carefully.

1. Account Registration

To use our services, you must:

  • Be at least 18 years old or have parental consent
  • Provide accurate and complete registration information
  • Maintain the confidentiality of your account credentials
  • Notify us immediately of any unauthorized account activity

2. Service Description

MindSport Cameroon provides:

  • A platform connecting combat athletes with mental health professionals
  • Self-assessment tools and wellness tracking features
  • Educational resources about mental health in sports

3. User Responsibilities

You agree to:

MindSport Cameroon:

Cookie Policy

This Cookie Policy explains how MindSport Cameroon uses cookies and similar technologies when you visit our platform.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit websites. They help the website remember information about your visit.

2. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for:

  • Essential Cookies: Necessary for platform functionality (e.g., login sessions)
  • Performance Cookies: Help us understand how users interact with our platform
  • Functional Cookies: Remember your preferences and settings
  • Analytics Cookies: Allow us to improve our services by analyzing usage patterns

3. Third-Party Cookies

We may use services that set cookies, including:

  • Google Analytics for usage statistics
  • Payment processors for transaction security
  • Social media platforms for sharing features (if enabled)

4. Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. However, disabling essential cookies may affect platform functionality.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy as our practices evolve. Significant changes will be communicated through our platform.
