About MindSport Cameroon

Pioneering mental health support for combat athletes across Cameroon.

Our Story

MindSport Cameroon was founded in 2023 by a dedicated team of sports psychologists, former combat athletes, and mental health advocates who recognized a critical gap in support services for athletes in Cameroon.

After witnessing talented athletes struggle with performance anxiety, competition stress, and post-defeat recovery, we committed to creating a comprehensive mental wellness platform specifically tailored to the unique challenges faced by combat athletes.

Today, MindSport Cameroon serves hundreds of athletes across boxing, wrestling, judo, taekwondo, MMA, and other combat sports, helping them develop mental resilience alongside physical strength.

Our Mission

To empower combat athletes in Cameroon with accessible, culturally-responsive mental health resources that enhance performance, wellbeing, and long-term career success.

Our Vision

To create a future where mental health support is integrated into every combat athlete's training in Cameroon, transforming the sports culture and producing champions who are as mentally resilient as they are physically strong.

Our Core Values

Accessibility

We believe quality mental health support should be available to all athletes regardless of economic background or location.

Cultural Relevance

Our approaches are rooted in understanding the specific cultural context of Cameroonian athletes and their communities.

Confidentiality

We maintain the highest standards of privacy and data protection, creating a safe space for athletes to be vulnerable.
