ABOUT US

Pioneering Mental Health Support For Combat Athletes

Transforming the mental wellness landscape for combat sports in Cameroon through innovative, culturally-relevant solutions.

Our Story

MindSport Cameroon was founded in 2023 by Dr. Adrien Mbarga, a former national boxing champion turned sports psychologist, who recognized a critical gap in mental health support services for athletes in Cameroon.

After years of witnessing talented athletes struggle with performance anxiety, competition stress, and post-defeat recovery, Dr. Mbarga assembled a team of mental health professionals, former athletes, and technologists to create a comprehensive mental wellness platform specifically tailored to the unique challenges faced by combat athletes.

Today, MindSport Cameroon serves hundreds of athletes across boxing, wrestling, judo, taekwondo, MMA, and other combat sports, helping them develop mental resilience alongside physical strength.

Our Headquarters

Yaound, Cameroon

Our Mission

To empower combat athletes in Cameroon with accessible, culturally-responsive mental health resources that enhance performance, wellbeing, and long-term career success. We aim to break the stigma around mental health in sports while providing evidence-based interventions tailored to the African athlete experience.

Our Vision

To create a future where mental health support is as integral to athlete development as physical training in Cameroon. We envision a sports culture that produces champions who are as mentally resilient as they are physically strong, with psychological wellbeing recognized as a cornerstone of athletic excellence.

Our Core Values

Accessibility

We believe quality mental health support should be available to all athletes regardless of economic background, location, or competitive level. Our tiered pricing and mobile-first approach ensure services reach athletes across Cameroon.

Cultural Relevance

Our approaches are rooted in understanding the specific cultural context of Cameroonian athletes. We blend global best practices with local traditions to create effective, culturally-appropriate interventions.

Confidentiality

We maintain the highest standards of privacy and data protection, creating a safe space for athletes to be vulnerable. Our platform uses end-to-end encryption and strict access controls to protect your information.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about MindSport Cameroon.

MindSport Cameroon is designed for combat athletes of all levels in Cameroon - from beginners to professionals. This includes practitioners of boxing, wrestling, judo, taekwondo, karate, MMA, and other martial arts. Coaches and trainers can also benefit from our resources to better support their athletes.

We offer tiered pricing to accommodate different needs and budgets:

  • Basic (free): Access to community forums and limited resources
  • Premium (15,000 XAF/month): Full access to all resources and discounted professional sessions
  • Professional (varies): Custom packages with dedicated mental coaches

We also offer scholarships for athletes with financial constraints.
