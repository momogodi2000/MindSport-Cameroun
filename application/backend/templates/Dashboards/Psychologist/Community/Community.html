Cultural Sensitivity Workshop

Douala, Cameroon - Conference Center
June 12-13, 2025 - 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM daily
8 CE Credits Available
About This Event

This two-day workshop will explore culturally sensitive approaches to mental health support for Cameroonian athletes. We'll examine traditional healing practices, local beliefs about mental health, and how to integrate these perspectives with evidence-based psychological interventions.

Special focus will be given to combat sports athletes and the unique cultural pressures they face in Cameroon. Case studies and interactive exercises will help participants develop practical skills for their practice.

Presenters

Dr. Atembe Enow

Clinical Psychologist specializing in sports and cultural psychology
